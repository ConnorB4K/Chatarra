Sticker Placeholders
====================
Place your sticker image files here with these exact filenames:

- laugh.webp    (Risa)
- thumbsup.webp (Aprobado)
- heart.webp    (Corazón)
- cry.webp      (Llorar)
- wow.webp      (Sorpresa)
- angry.webp    (Enojo)

Recommended: WebP or PNG format, 256x256 px or larger.
